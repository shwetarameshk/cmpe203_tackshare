About
What are the features of MyTacks?

Some of the main features of MyTacks are:

  1. Create Tacks
  2. Create Boards
  3. Follow Users
  4. Search for Users/Boards
  5. Mark Favorites
What is a tack?

A tack is a bookmark that contains an image or a video and a link to the website that you want to save.

What is a board?

A board is a collection of tacks. It can contain as many tacks as you wish to add. Generally, a board is used to organize tacks in a way that makes it easier for someone to find what they're looking for.

For example, you can store all bookmarks related to travel under a single board, and bookmarks related to your research paper under another board.

What does board privacy mean?

A board can be either Private or Public. A private board can be accessed by you and by those whom you choose to share it with. On the other hand, a public board can be searched and accessed by any user of MyTacks. Anyone could add a tack to your board.

You can change the privacy settings for your board at any time.

What does Follow mean?

To "Follow" a user means to get quick access to their public boards on your dashboard.

What are Favorite tacks?

A favorite tack is a tack that you would like to access quickly. When you open a tack, click on the star icon present on the top left corner of your tack to add/remove it from your list of Favorites
